## Converts array to the list with the links

**Definition:** arrayToHrefList(ArrayList stringList, ArrayList rowBrace = ["<li>", "</li>"], ArrayList listBrace = ["<ul style='list-style-type:none'>","</ul>"])

**Usage:** arrayToHrefList(["A", "B", "C"], ["<li>", "</li>"], ["<ul style='list-style-type:none'>", "</ul>"])
